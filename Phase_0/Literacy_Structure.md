## Phase_0 - Literacy 

**Description:** The Phase_0 submodule is structured as a engineering journal for a student which is used assemble and access notes, documents or other references that would be useful for further understanding. 

**Structure:** Phase_0 is broken down into seven (7) sections of learning which are listed below: 
> 1. Systems Thinking
> 2. Math Language
> 3. Physics Intuition
> 4. Electronics Literacy
> 5. Mechanical Literacy
> 6. Control Feedback
> 7. Safety Failure

Each of these sections will have a submodule under the Phase_0 submodule and will have, minimum, a submodule for notes, references, and planning respectively.
